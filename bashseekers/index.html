About Us

Built from friends, driving, and local shredders.

HOW IT STARTED

Bashseekers started in early 2019 as a couple of friends pushing the limits of their driving. Artwork and digital design were always part of the vision, with the idea to grow Bashseekers beyond just stickers over time.

WHAT IT IS

The main focus is still driving — raw, aggressive, and exhilarating street style shared with talented local shredders. Bashseekers is built around that underground drift scene, the people around it, and the things created from it.

Keep driving. Keep creating. Keep seeking.
