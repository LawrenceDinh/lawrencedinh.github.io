Carrot Stew

This emaculate stew is one of our most popular dishes and is beloved by many! The meal consists of carrots, goat butter, fresh milk and tabantha wheat. This is one of our oldest menu items, and has been eaten by multiple famous people, including Link himself!

Meat and Rice Bowl

This seemingly boring meat and rice dish may seem ordinary to those who haven't visited the Restaurant. But this dish is highly recommended by our regulars. It consists of three juicy pieces of tender meat, famous hylian rice and our special seasoning. This is our recommendation for any newcomers to the restaurant to begin their journey in delicious, family friendly meals

Clam Chowder

Hyrule Restaurant attracts multiple people from all over Hyrule, but the Zora are most fond of this Clam Chowder meal. Our fresh ingredients shine through the most with this dish! These fresh ingredients are: hearty blueshell snail, fresh milk, goat butter and tabantha wheat.
